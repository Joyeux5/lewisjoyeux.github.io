Ammonite is a small animation studio based in Glasgow focusing on creative and let's be honest, pretty quirky 3D animation (Check out our work page if you haven't already). We love adding wit and whimsy into our work and with our creative approach to animation, we aim to produce something truly unique on every project. 

Ammonite was founded by Glen Johnston and Thomas Salgarella and together they lead a small team of talented artists with the collective goal of creating great animations and more importantly a great studio.
