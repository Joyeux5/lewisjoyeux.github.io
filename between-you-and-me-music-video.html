Between You and Me (Music Video)

We were lucky enough to provide all VFX for the "Between You and Me" music video by One Bit and Louisa. 
Give it a quick watch then check out all the work we put in to produce the final effects.
The Director wanted to go with an abstract geometric pattern that ca equally represent the 2 prevalent colours in the video so I set about creating some concepts experimenting with the density and detail.
At one point in the video we needed a more organic explosion of colour so we started experimenting with animating and distorting procedural textures to create these effects.
I finished up with RnD, filmed the video and moved into post and world of rotoscoping.
