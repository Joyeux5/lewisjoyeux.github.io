Taps Aff

In Glasgow, there is a cultural phenomenon called "Taps Aff". It seems to be some sort of strict ritual performed by the Glaswegians whenever the sun is visible and normally ends in catastrophe. We tried Our very best to capture what it is like during this rare event.
