Fresh Car Valet Animation

When we were asked to make an animation for a car valet company we really did not expect a brief like this. It was a brave move having cute characters sing along to a whimsical tune only to then kill them all and use that to advertise your service on national television but that is the type of clients we love.
If anyone else wants to make an animation where we kill all your characters drop me a message! I mean, I guess you can also drop me a message if you want an animation without a load of violence in it I suppose...
Thanks to STV Creative for getting us on board on this project, it is not one we will forget soon.

We also did some character tests to test out the rigs and we can never turn down an opportunity to violently kill our own creations.
