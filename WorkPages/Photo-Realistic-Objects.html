Photo-Realistic Objects

Corona Extra

A 3d Render i created with Blender and composited in Photoshop
I wanted to see if i could create a realistic advetisemnet similar to what you would see on TV.

I used an edited glass shader for the material of the bottle and used a curv to controll the reflections of the lights i added into the scene.

The condesation was created by adding a nomal map to the glass shader.
and finally the water effect around the bottle i did in post production in photoshop i could have done a liquid simulation in blender however i thought it was a bit extreme for a simple freeze frame, on the other hand if i was doing an animation a liquid simulation would be more appropriate.

Au Vodka

A 3d Render I created with Blender and composited in Photoshop.
For this projects I used custom normal maps that I had created myself in photoshop, it was something ive never had to do before so i was unsure if it would work.
after creating the bottle I used a simple metalic gold shader and added the smoke in photoshop.
