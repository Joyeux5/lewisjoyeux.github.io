Anthem Helmet Build

We had the pleasure of working with Sorenzo Studios to help bring this monster of a helmet to life.
We were in charge of taking the high poly 3D models from EA and making them into 3D printable pieces for assembly.  We also worked alongside Optimistic Geometry to make sure the faceplate could open up revealing your giddy wee face.
Sorenzo Studios wanted a way to display the helmet so I designed a custom stand that could house internal components for the smoke and lighting effects which makes the whole thing like 72% more badass.
